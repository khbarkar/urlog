SecFlow

Optional security-flow for Urlog. SecFlow pours third-party security signals into Integration, Delivery, Debt, and Eye without becoming a SAST platform or owning release policy.

What It Does

Prompt Risk

LlamaFirewall / PromptGuard-style prompt-injection and jailbreak findings.

runtime

Regression Evidence

garak-style CI and red-team evidence for security release gates.

CI

Supply Chain

Dependency, image, SBOM, deprecation, obsolete runtime, and license findings.

findings

Boundaries

SecFlow emits scored findings and gate recommendations. Integration imports them into readiness scoring, Delivery consumes them as gate evidence, Debt stores them as incident/audit evidence, and Eye reports them.

General code scanning is out of scope unless a customer explicitly plugs in a tool that produces compatible findings.
