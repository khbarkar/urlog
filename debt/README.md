# Debt

Norn name: **Urdr**  
Small name: **Acts**

Debt owns what happened, why it happened, and what actions are allowed next. It keeps the forensic record, incident lifecycle, audit evidence, and intervention risk scoring for autonomous operations.

## Owns

- Session forensics.
- Incident lifecycle.
- Hash-chained immutable audit records.
- AI Act Article 12 style retention and logging evidence.
- Intervention risk scoring.
- Human approval routing for predefined destructive actions.

## Does Not Own

- Ingestion and CI/source signal collection. That is Integration.
- Release and SLO gate policy. That is Delivery.
- Optional security scanner internals. That is Eye.

## ForgeBoard Role

Debt diagnoses ForgeBoard incidents such as stream lag, bad transaction-policy uploads, dependency regressions, or failed canaries. It scores proposed actions and records why an action was automated or held for human approval.
