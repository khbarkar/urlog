# 03 - Phoenix

Goal: self-host Arize Phoenix side by side with Langfuse and understand its trace/eval model.

## Try

1. Run Phoenix locally with Docker or Python, following the official Phoenix docs.
2. Configure the same instrumented app to export traces to Phoenix.
3. Send the same retrieval-nord scenario you sent to Langfuse.

## Learn

Record:

- How Phoenix models traces and spans.
- How datasets and evals relate to traces.
- How Phoenix handles OpenInference and OTel data.
- What questions Phoenix answers better than Langfuse.
- What remains missing for autonomous SRE/SecOps.
